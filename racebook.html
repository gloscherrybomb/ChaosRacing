Series Overview

Each week one of the iconic British Cycling Circuit Series courses will be available to race using TrainingPeaks Virtual's new GPXplore functionality.

Each course GPX is carefully curated to best replicate the real life courses and ensure no course oddities.

These routes are characterised by tight turns, steep climbs and constant on/off action. The series culminates with the iconic Lincoln Grand Prix and the brutally steep Michaelgate cobbled climb.

Choose your own slot: with races twice per day for a whole week, you can choose when to take part!

Ruleset

  • Performance verification is turned on. Smart trainer and heartrate monitor are mandatory. The system decision is final.
  • Pens are determined by your ARR rank
  • Bots will fill pens (to 30 racers)
  • GC times are decided on the gap from the winner. The winner scores a penalty of 0 seconds, if 2nd place is 10 seconds back, they will receive a +10s score
  • Races are scheduled every day 2 slots: 1PM UK time (BST/GMT) and 6:30PM UK time (BST/GMT). Only the first race that you take part in per course counts for the GC. You are welcome to take part multiple times during the week that a specific course is available.
  • In the case that a rider is promoted between ranks during the course of the series, their score will carry over to the higher rank. For example, if a bronze rider places 2nd in their first race with a time deficit of 5 seconds, and is subsequently promoted to silver, in the second race they will be placed in the silver pen, and their silver GC score is +5.
